The IVF challenge

IVF can retrieve, fertilize and transfer. It cannot, by itself, confer competence on an embryo.

The meaningful outcome is live birth per intention to treat, read together with risks, cost, time invested and patient experience. ForEve addresses the upstream biological constraint: age related decline in oocyte quality.

Strategic memo

Boost success without breaking the IVF chain.

EpicOva is built as an add on to the fertility clinic workflow. The aim is not to replace IVF, but to improve the biological quality of the starting material before fertilization.

Better

More mature eggs. The product thesis is to recover immature or unstable oocytes by improving cellular quality before IVF or ICSI.

Faster

Shorter IVF journey. More usable oocytes per retrieval may reduce the need for repeated stimulation and retrieval cycles.

Cheaper

Lower cost per baby. The business logic targets time to baby, dollars per baby and life disruption.

Science

Targeting the root cause of egg aging.

ForEve’s research links oocyte aging to heterochromatin loss, retrotransposon activation and DNA damage. The therapeutic logic is to restore chromatin regulation, repress retrotransposons and improve oocyte function.

Mechanism

With age, oocytes lose heterochromatin structure. This weakens silencing and transcriptional control, which can impair cellular function.

Damage

Retrotransposon activity increases in old oocytes and can contribute to DNA damage and loss of cellular functionality.

Recovery

ForEve’s preclinical work aims to enhance heterochromatin and reduce retrotransposon expression in aged oocytes.

Platform

From EpicOva to an epigenetic cell rejuvenation platform.

The first program targets oocytes because the unmet need is clear, measurable and deeply human. The broader platform vision extends to T cell augmentation, tissue transplantation and ocular dystrophy after validation of the core rejuvenation logic.

01

Restore heterochromatin

Target epigenetic modifiers to rebuild chromatin organization in aged cells.

02

Repress retrotransposons

Reduce harmful retrotransposon activity associated with loss of oocyte function.

03

Improve cell quality

Measure whether treated oocytes show improved maturation and downstream fertilization potential.

04

Fit existing IVF

Supplement the in vitro maturation culture medium with EpicOva, without disrupting the core clinical chain.
